Intro

The year is 1014.
The people are speaking of a terrible evil spreading from Altamore region. Four wizards have gone missing. They were exploring a dungeon full of vile creatures the world had never seen before. You, a brave hero of Eteria have taken the most dangerous quest yet - putting an end to the unknown evil.

As you aproach the darkest place your eyes have ever seen, you feel as if your soul is changing. Your sight begins to dim. The robes on you, your sword and other items you haven't seen start to glow. Your heart starts craving for loot and sensing it through the fog.

Will you be able to stop this corruption and save your remaining humanity?! Are you brave enough to venture into the dungeon?!

Neutral Ending

As you combine all 4 MacGuffins your soul begins to shift. The goodness in you purges the source of corruption, although not without taking a toll.

The world feels as a great burden falls off of its shoulders, but the hero is never seen again.

Evil ending

As you combine all 4 MacGuffins your soul begins to shift. The corruption in you summons the great evil. Your crave for power plunges the world in darkness!
